Triangle Tracker

This is a simple web-app that allows a person to determine the type of Triangle by entering its measurements. Please try it out




There are various shapes that exists. However, the triangle is the most challenging shape to identify depending on its properies. Accordingly, mathematicians have been able to record three types of triangles depending on its measurements. The scalene triangle, the Isosceles triangle and the Equirateral triangle. The calculator above helps in identifying these three types of triangles. Althought there exist more.
